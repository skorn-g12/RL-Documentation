Orion.AI
====================================
The final goal is to be able to come up with a myriad of Minecraft bots that do specialized tasks.  
But let's start at the beginning first. We will be using `OpenAI's gym <https://gymnasium.farama.org/>`_

.. note::
   All development of Gym has been moved to Gymnasium, a new package in the Farama Foundation that's maintained 
   by the same team of developers who have maintained Gym for the past 18 months.
